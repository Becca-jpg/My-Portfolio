About Me


I attended General Assembly in Chicago for their Front End Web Development bootcamp. There I learned how to build a website from scratch and the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We also spent time learning about GitHub and how to use it to benefit ourselves as well as others.


Our final project was to build a complete website and have version 1 ready by the last day. I chose to make a website for a colleage of mine who runs a small make-up buisness. This allowed me to get an idea of what it is like to build a website for a client that solves the problems they face and looks the way they want it to. Now, I feel ready to take on new projects with new clients.


I have completed my bootcamp but my learning is far from over. I am still studying code on the side, especially JavaScript, and I want to continue taking on new projects so I can apply all the skills I have to real world problems.
